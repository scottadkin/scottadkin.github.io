Power Multiplier
Global power scale, higher the value the more power every car will have.
Gravity
Global gravity.
Cash
Force your career profile cash to this value.
Drag Scale
Global drag force applied to all cars, if you use a negative value cars will speed up the faster they go until the game crashes, higher values make it harder to reach max speeds.
Gear Ratio Scale
This affects all cars gear ratios, the lower the percentage the longer the gears will be, I recommened 80% or 90% if you use a power multiplier > 200%.
Tyre Wear Scale
This affects all cars tyre wear, the lower the percentage the longer the tyres last.
